**📋 Problem Statement**

In engineering colleges, students often appear for campus placements without a clear understanding of the skills required for their desired job roles. There is no structured system to help students identify the gap between their current skill set and the skills demanded by the industry. This lack of awareness leads to poor placement preparation, low confidence, and high rejection rates during campus recruitment. Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent web-based system that allows students to enter their skills, compare them against job role requirements, visualize their skill gaps, and predict their placement probability using Machine Learning — enabling students to prepare effectively and improve their placement outcomes.

**PROJECT OBJECTIVES**

1. To develop a web-based system that allows students to register and manage their skill profiles.

2. To enable students to select their desired job role and compare their skills against industry requirements.

3. To automatically identify and display the skill gap between student skills and job requirements.

4. To predict student placement probability using Machine Learning (Logistic Regression).

5. To provide personalized course recommendations for missing skills.

6. To help placement officers monitor student placement readiness.
